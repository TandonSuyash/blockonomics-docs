## The simplest way to accept Bitcoin

Blockonomics is a **non-custodial** Bitcoin payment processor. That means when your customer pays, the Bitcoin goes straight into your wallet — Blockonomics never holds your funds.

## How it works

Blockonomics uses your wallet's **xPub key** (Master Public Key) to generate a unique Bitcoin address for every transaction. This means:

- Payments go **directly to your wallet** — we never touch them
- Every customer gets a **unique address**, so payments are never mixed up
- You receive funds **instantly**, with no withdrawal delays

<Info>
  Blockonomics works with any Bitcoin wallet that exposes an xPub key — including Ledger, Electrum, and most hardware wallets. [See supported wallets →](/wallets/overview)
</Info>
