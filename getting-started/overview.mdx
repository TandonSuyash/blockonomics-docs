Blockonomics is a non-custodial Bitcoin payment processor. When your customer pays, 
the crypto goes straight into **your** wallet. Blockonomics never holds your funds, 
never touches your revenue, and never asks you to trust us with your money.

Think of us as the bridge between your customer and your wallet. We handle the 
technical layer, generating addresses, detecting payments, notifying your store. 
While you stay in full control of your funds at all times.

## Who is Blockonomics for?

Whether you're a store owner who just wants a "Pay with Bitcoin" button on your 
WordPress site, or a developer building a custom payment flow from scratch.
Blockonomics works for both.

- **Store owners** can get started in under 10 minutes using our plugins for 
WordPress, WHMCS, or PrestaShop — no coding required.
- **Developers** can integrate directly using our REST API for full control over 
the payment experience.

| Currency | Symbol | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bitcoin | BTC | Supported across all integrations |
| Bitcoin Cash | BCH | Supported on WordPress, WHMCS, PrestaShop |
| Tether | USDT | Supported via API and select integrations |

## How it works

Blockonomics uses your wallet's **xPub key** (also called a Master Public Key) to 
generate a unique payment address for every transaction. Here's why that matters:

- Every customer gets a **unique address** so payments are never mixed up
- Money goes **directly to your wallet** the moment payment is made
- Even if Blockonomics went offline, your funds are already safe in your wallet

<Info>
Not familiar with xPub keys? Don't worry, our wallet guides explain everything 
from scratch. You don't need to understand the technical details to get started.
</Info>

## Before you dive in

There are three things every Blockonomics setup needs, regardless of which 
integration you use:

<Steps>
  <Step title="A Blockonomics account">
    Free to create, no identity verification required. 
    [Create your account →](/getting-started/create-account)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A Bitcoin wallet">
    You need a wallet that can show its xPub key, such as Ledger or Electrum. 
    This is where your payments will land. 
    [See supported wallets →](/wallets/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A store">
    A "store" in Blockonomics links your wallet to your website. Each store has 
    a callback URL, this is how your site gets notified when a payment arrives. 
    [Learn how stores work →](/getting-started/add-store)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Ready? Start with [creating your account →](/getting-started/create-account)
